How to Play

Don't Miss is a basketball shot game created using HTML, Canvas, and Vanilla Javascript. Players can play a easy to pick up game where they can throw a ball into a hoop to score points using their mouse to the views of the beautiful Venice Beach basketball courts.


Legend has it, the one who can flick the ball with their mouse can enter a very secret society. A society of elite ballers. Some say if you can get the elusive 99 points highscore. you unlock the knowledge to Lebron James secret to eternal youth. Think you have what it takes yungblud?
